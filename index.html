Feed The Dog

Catch the yummies!
   (bones, balls, bacon, socks).

Shoot the yuckies!
   (broccoli, chili peppers, lemons).

If a yucky touches you... game over.

CONTROLS

Left Arrow: Move Left
Right Arrow: Move Right
Space Bar: Fire

Scoring

Catch a yummy: +5 pts
Shoot a yummy: -3 pts
Let a yummy go by: -1 pts

Catch a yucky: Game Over!
Shoot a yucky: +10 pts
Let a yucky go by: -3 pts
